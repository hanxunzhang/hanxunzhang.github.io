Hanson's Life Story

Chapter 1.
A Visionary Shanghainese

1994 - 2012

Born and raised in Shanghai, I am grateful to spend the first eighteen years of my life in one of the most international and dynamic cities in the world, a place I always call home. Growing up here, I have met people from different cultures and backgrounds all over the world, which allows me to form a worldview at a very young age.

Chapter 2.
Cornellian v1.0 - Hotelie for Life

2012 - 2016

I came to the US to study Hotel Administration at Cornell. My four years as a Cornellian has left me with an unforgettable memory and shaped me greatly to become the person I am today.

Chapter 2. Cornellian v1.0 - Hotelie for Life

Ye Hosts Honorary Society

It was an honor for me to be invited into the Ye Hosts Honorary Society, where the brightest Hotel School students gather together into a cohesive unit that acts as role model to other students and serves the needs of Hotel School students, faculty and staff.

American Hotel & Lodging Association

American Hotel & Lodging Association (AHLA) is a professional organization that represents every segment of the hotel industry; I co-founded its student chapter at Cornell University, helped it win the “2016 Student Club of the Year” award by AHLA, and continue to participate in industry events and conferences after graduation.

Chapter 3. An Active New Yorker

2016 - 2018

Associate Manager at The Leading Hotels of the World

Working in the corporate office of a luxury hospitality company in NYC enables me to work collaboratively with a diverse group of fabulous people around the globe who not only have business acumen but also appreciate the beauty of life.

Alumni Events and Activities

NYC is also a place where I can take full advantage of connecting with other Cornell alumni and expanding my professional network.

2018 - 2020

Through my MBA at Johnson, I want to explore more about the general business world and transition into a more financial related and analytical role. I can't wait to start my new journey in this place just across the Statler Hall!
